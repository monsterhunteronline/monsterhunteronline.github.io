The Weapon Stone (F7)

The weapon stone is a grindy (and expensive) feature that can give you great benefits once you understand how to make the most out of it.

To open it, press F7.


  1. Stone Level
    Max Level: 150
  2. Stone Experience
    Accumulating enough stone experience points will increase the stone level, points are earned using the gray stones.
  3. Available Points for using
    Silver points are got by leveling up the stone.
    Gold points are got by events, cash shop or doing lvl 90 unstable elite hunts (2 per hunt, max of 3 hunts per day).
  4. Reset Button
    Allows you to reset points currently allocated at a cost of 2000 cash shop tickets.
  5. Silver Points Area.
  6. Gold Points Area.
  7. Unavailable Area as of Now.
  8. Summary of currently accumulated bonuses.

At first, your weapon stone will look like this. To unlock the second tier of skills, you need to have invested 15 points in the first tier and then press the button.

The unlocking of a tier will increase the max numbers of points allocatable for the skills of the previous tiers by 5. From tier 2 onwards, the Gold Point Zone skills have to be unlocked by allocating enough points in the silver point zone, after which they can be unlocked.

Tier 1 skills

Increase physical defense, not very useful except for teching into the second tier when you have a low amounts of gold points.

Increase attack raw, best t1 skills but uses gold points.

Tier 2 skills

Increases attack raw, best silver zone skill.

Increase physical defense, okish filler skill.

Increase movement speed WHILE IN TOWN. Unless you are about to uninstall based on how slow your character moves in town, skip this.

Requires 15 points in t2 skills to unlock. Requires Gold Points.
Great skill which gives crit, but kind of expensive.

Tier 3 skills

The tier 3 status triplets are lacklusters. They give very small bonuses to status weapons and thus are unnoticeable. Since poison is all but useless, if you are using ponts to unlock the Gold Point Zone skill use sleep and paralysis.

Increases elemental damage, but it suffers from the t3 curse of being practically unnoticeable. If you need to dump points, this will do.

Requires 15 points in t3 skills to unlock. Requires Gold Points.
Increases the attack raw of skills IF they crit, which makes it less reliable than your other 3 damage increasing skills in the weapon stone. If you are rich and plan to send some Tencent's employee's son to college, you could get this
